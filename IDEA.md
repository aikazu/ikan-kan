# Fish Empire: An Incremental Game Concept

## Core Concept
Start with a single fish in a tiny bowl. Click to feed your fish, earning Fish Points (FP) and occasionally triggering fish reproduction. Upgrade to larger aquariums, automate feeding and breeding, expand to multiple locations, and eventually build a global aquatic empire spanning oceans and even alien worlds.

## Core Gameplay Loop

### Clicking Phase (Initial)
- **Action**: Click the "Feed Fish" button
- **Feedback**: Each click:
  - Earns 1 Fish Point (FP)
  - Has a 5% chance to spawn a new fish (max capacity limited by tank size)
  - Simple visual of fish swimming toward food, bubble animation, and "plop" sound
- **Goal**: Accumulate enough FP to buy your first automated feeder or tank upgrade

### Expansion Phase (Core Loop)
- **Spend FP to expand your aquatic ecosystem**:
  - **Tank Upgrades**:
    - Fish Bowl (Starting): Holds 3 fish
    - Small Aquarium (Costs 50 FP): Holds 10 fish
    - Large Aquarium (Costs 250 FP): Holds 30 fish
    - Home Pond (Costs 1,000 FP): Holds 100 fish
    - Indoor Reef System (Costs 5,000 FP): Holds 300 fish
    - Private Lake (Costs 25,000 FP): Holds 1,000 fish
    - Commercial Aquarium (Costs 100,000 FP): Holds 3,000 fish
    - Marine Research Center (Costs 500,000 FP): Holds 10,000 fish
    - Ocean Sanctuary (Costs 2,500,000 FP): Holds 50,000 fish
    - Artificial Sea (Costs 10,000,000 FP): Holds 200,000 fish
    - Continental Water System (Costs 100,000,000 FP): Holds 1,000,000 fish
    - Planetary Ocean Network (Costs 1B FP): Holds 10,000,000 fish
    - Extraterrestrial Fish Colonies (Costs 100B FP): Holds 100,000,000 fish
  
  - **Passive FP Generation**: Each fish generates 0.1 FP per second
  - **Breeding Mechanic**: More fish = faster breeding potential
  - **Feedback**: Visual representation of your expanding aquatic system, more fish swimming, counter climbing faster
  - **Goal**: Balance purchasing tank upgrades to increase fish capacity with other improvements

### Automation Phase
- **Spend FP on automation**:
  - Auto-Feeder (Costs 100 FP): Feeds fish once every 10 seconds (0.1 feeds/sec)
  - Timed Feeder (Costs 500 FP): Feeds fish once every 5 seconds (0.2 feeds/sec)
  - Digital Feeding System (Costs 2,500 FP): Feeds fish once per second (1 feed/sec)
  - Fish AI Caretaker (Costs 10,000 FP): Feeds fish 5 times per second (5 feeds/sec)
  - Automated Ecosystem (Costs 50,000 FP): Feeds fish 20 times per second (20 feeds/sec)
  - Quantum Feed Distributor (Costs 500,000 FP): Feeds fish 100 times per second (100 feeds/sec)
  - (Continue with increasingly powerful and expensive automation options)

### Multi-Location Expansion
- **After reaching Commercial Aquarium level, unlock multiple locations**:
  - Second Location (Costs 150,000 FP): Duplicate your setup with 50% starting efficiency
  - Third Location (Costs 750,000 FP): Unlock third site with 50% starting efficiency
  - Fourth Location (Costs 3,000,000 FP): Unlock fourth site with 50% starting efficiency
  - (Continue with more locations, each more expensive than the last)
  - Each location can be upgraded separately, creating parallel progression paths
  - Eventually unlock synergies between locations (e.g., breeding programs, rare fish trading)

### Species Diversification
- **Collect different fish species with unique benefits**:
  - Guppies (Starting): Basic fish (1× breeding rate)
  - Goldfish (Costs 500 FP): 1.5× FP generation
  - Angelfish (Costs 2,500 FP): 2× breeding rate
  - Clownfish (Costs 10,000 FP): Occasionally generates "Lucky Bubbles" (like golden cookies)
  - Discus (Costs 50,000 FP): Attracts visitors (new income stream)
  - Exotic Rarities (Costs 250,000+ FP): Various special abilities and massive FP multipliers
  - Genetic Hybrids (Costs 10,000,000+ FP): Custom fish with combined abilities

### Upgrade Phase (Enhancement)
- **Spend FP on system upgrades**:
  - **Feeding Upgrades**:
    - Premium Fish Food (Costs 200 FP): Clicking produces 2 FP and 7% breeding chance
    - Nutritional Supplements (Costs 1,000 FP): Clicking produces 5 FP and 10% breeding chance
    - Growth Hormones (Costs 5,000 FP): Auto-Feeders are 2× more effective
    - Genetic Optimization (Costs 25,000 FP): Fish breeding rate increased by 50%
  
  - **Environmental Upgrades**:
    - Water Purifier (Costs 500 FP): Increases all fish HP generation by 10%
    - Temperature Control (Costs 2,500 FP): Increases breeding success rate by 25%
    - Oxygen Infusion (Costs 10,000 FP): Fish FP generation increased by 25%
    - Artificial Reefs (Costs 50,000 FP): Increases tank capacity by 20%
  
  - **Management Upgrades**:
    - Assistant Manager (Costs 5,000 FP): Automation runs 10% faster
    - Breeding Program (Costs 20,000 FP): Each fish has a small chance to produce a rare variant
    - International Markets (Costs 100,000 FP): All FP generation increased by 20%
    - Global Supply Chain (Costs 500,000 FP): All costs reduced by 5%
  
  - **Global Boost Upgrades**:
    - Fisheries Refinement I (Costs 1,000,000 FP): All production increased by 25%
    - Fisheries Refinement II (Costs 10,000,000 FP): All production increased by 50%
    - (Higher tier global upgrades with massive effects)

## Special Mechanics

### Lucky Bubbles (Golden Cookie equivalent)
- **Mechanic**: Occasionally, colorful bubbles appear floating across the screen
- **Action**: Pop them before they disappear
- **Rewards**:
  - "Feeding Frenzy": +1000% clicking power for 30 seconds
  - "Breeding Boom": Massive temporary boost to breeding chance
  - "Fish Shower": Instantly gain X seconds of production
  - "School's In": All fish produce double FP for 2 minutes
  - "Visitor Rush": Instant cash bonus (if you've unlocked visitors)

### Visitors & Tourism
- **Unlocked after reaching Home Pond level**
- **Mechanic**: People visit your aquatic displays
- **Benefits**:
  - Generate a secondary currency (Tickets) based on fish variety and rarity
  - Spend Tickets on exclusive upgrades unavailable with FP
  - Occasional "VIP Visitors" grant special bonuses

### Expeditions System
- **Unlocked after reaching Marine Research Center level**
- **Mechanic**: Send teams to discover new fish species in remote locations
- **Function**:
  - Each expedition costs resources but can bring back rare species
  - Time-based mini-game within the main game loop
  - Higher risk expeditions bring more valuable rewards

### Prestige System ("Conservation Grants")
- **Mechanic**: Once reaching massive production, option to "donate" your fish empire to conservation
- **Reward**: Gain permanent "Conservation Influence" points
- **Effects**:
  - Each point gives +1% to all FP production forever
  - Unlocks special facilities and species in future playthroughs
  - Grants access to exclusive Prestige Upgrades

### Late-Game Content
- **Scientific Breakthrough Tree**:
  - Research genetic modifications for super-fish
  - Develop new tank technologies
  - Discover evolutionary secrets
- **Ocean Colonization**:
  - Extend your empire to wild oceans
  - Manage ecosystems on a planetary scale
- **Space Aquatics**:
  - Discover alien fish species
  - Build massive orbital aquariums
  - Terraform alien worlds for aquatic life

## Addiction Factors (Why It Works)
- **Organic Growth Theme**: Watching your fish multiply provides a natural sense of progression
- **Varied Visual Feedback**: Diverse fish species, tank upgrades, and environments provide visual rewards
- **Multiple Progression Paths**: Tank size, automation, species, locations - many ways to advance
- **Breeding Chance**: Random breeding adds excitement to clicking (will this click make a new fish?)
- **Multi-dimensional Scaling**: Not just more fish, but more tanks, more locations, more species
- **Collection Appeal**: Gotta catch 'em all mentality with different fish species
- **Simulation Satisfaction**: Creating thriving ecosystems appeals to nurturing instincts
- **Exponential Growth**: Numbers quickly balloon from single digits to billions
- **Manageable Complexity**: Simple to start, with depth emerging gradually

- **Dynamic Events**: Random events like disease outbreaks or algae blooms that require attention
